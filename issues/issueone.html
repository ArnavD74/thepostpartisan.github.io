The Postpartisan: Issue One

August 24, 2020

Thank you for reading the inaugral issue of the Postpartisan. This publication is our first step towards the goal laid out in "A Statement of Intent": a society of decreased polarization, where people with genuine disagreements can actually participate in dialogue, in the hopes of reducing dogma and better solving the many issues which impact our society. To this end, we encourage you to read and contend with those essays which may disagree with you ideologically - this challenge may get you thinking differently, or at least better understanding what you already believed. Respectful, civil, and substantive comments are encouraged.

Do you have extensive disagreements with a particular essay, or want to build off of someone's ideas? Our very goal is to build a Great Conversation between left and right across future issues. We may even have a future issue just for rebuttals. Thus, we encourage you to write up your ideas, and go to the 'Join Us' page to find how to submit your essay. As is said in one of our core principles, discourse above all.

From all founders of the Postpartisan, thank you for your support.
